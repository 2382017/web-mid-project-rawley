my story during the boring vacation right

don't have many interesting things to tell you about my vacation. I just woke up, ate, drank, rested for a while waiting for 8 o'clock to catch fish in the river, when 8 o'clock arrived my brother and I went to catch fish using nets, after netting we were very happy because we got a lot of fish, finally we went home after feeling enough and

asked my mother to cook the fish, after cooking and we had lunch with a side dish of fish that I got with my brother. After eating I slept, and waited to work loading palm oil after the time came I left for the field by car, as soon as I was in the field I started my work loading palm oil into the car, after the car was

full I took the TBS to the ram to be weighed and after weighing I unloaded the fruit and I paid my members who worked, after I finished around 4 o'clock I continued to play volleyball with my friends, long story short it was late I went home and showered, ate, I continued to gather with my friends long story short it was 11 o'clock time to go home because I had been called from home.
